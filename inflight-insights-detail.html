InFlight Insights: Sustainable Aviation

A strategic business venture developed for the ASU Technology Innovation Lab, designed to solve in-flight cabin waste through AI-driven predictive analytics and pitched directly to Honeywell Aerospace leadership.

The Innovation Challenge

The Technology Innovation Lab at ASU, in partnership with Honeywell Aerospace, presented a unique challenge: develop a viable business solution to a pressing problem in the aviation industry.

This highly selective program accepted only **23 students from over 700 applicants**. As one of the chosen participants, I joined a team to compete against 8 other groups in a semester-long battle of innovation, strategy, and execution.

From Problem to Product

Our team identified a multi-billion dollar problem: in-flight food waste.

The Problem

Airlines waste billions annually on untouched food and beverages due to inefficient, fixed-inventory systems. This overstocking leads to financial loss, increased fuel burn from excess weight, and significant environmental impact.

Our Solution: InFlight Insights

A B2B SaaS platform using AI and advanced sensors to provide predictive data analytics. Our system captures real-time data on food consumption, allowing airlines to optimize catering orders, reduce waste, and cut operational costs without disrupting cabin crew workflow.
